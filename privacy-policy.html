Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This privacy policy applies to applications and tools developed by Aaryan Tibrewal that use the LinkedIn API, including any integrations built for personal development, research, or portfolio purposes.

1. Information we access

When you authorise this application with LinkedIn, we may access the following information from your LinkedIn profile depending on the permissions granted:

  • Your basic profile information (name, headline, profile photo)
  • Your LinkedIn member ID
  • Email address (if the r_emailaddress scope is granted)
  • Content sharing permissions (if the w_member_social scope is granted)

2. How we use this information

Information accessed through the LinkedIn API is used solely for:

  • Personal development and portfolio management purposes
  • Automating content publishing to your own LinkedIn profile
  • Demonstrating API integration capabilities as part of a developer portfolio

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

3. Data storage

This application does not store your LinkedIn data on any external server. Any data accessed through the LinkedIn API is processed in memory during the session only and is not persisted, shared, or sold to any third party.

4. Data sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties. Your LinkedIn data is never disclosed outside of the application session.

5. Third-party services

This application uses the LinkedIn API. Your use of LinkedIn is governed by LinkedIn's Privacy Policy.

6. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information accessed during API sessions. OAuth 2.0 is used for all LinkedIn authentication — we never see or store your LinkedIn password.

7. Your rights

You can revoke this application's access to your LinkedIn account at any time by visiting LinkedIn Permitted Services and removing the app.

8. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
